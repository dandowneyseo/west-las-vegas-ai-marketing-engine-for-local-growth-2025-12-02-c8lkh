Absolutely. Our AI is industry-agnostic and has been successfully deployed across real estate, home services, fitness, dental, legal services, automotive, retail, and many other industries. Our AI is trained on advanced language models that understand context and nuance. For most common questions, the AI provides immediate, accurate answers. You maintain full control over escalation rules and thresholds.
